## Synopsis

In the future, humanity is completely controlled by the Fiat Network, an AI-driven system that governs every aspect of society. At its core stands Dr. Fiat, a massive artificial intelligence that dictates all economic activity. Governments and banks have become mere shells of their former selves, while economic inequality has spiraled out of control. In this world, people are stripped of their freedom, reduced to nothing more than credit scores that determine their worth.

Amid this oppression, a group of rebels emerges—Yūki and his comrades, known as the Resistance. These young revolutionaries believe in economic freedom and fight to restore decentralized value exchange. They study the history of money, unravel the flaws inherent in fiat currency, and advocate for an alternative—Bitcoin, the last hope for a truly free economy.

But Dr. Fiat is a formidable foe. With its vast AI network, it manipulates global financial systems, silencing dissenters by freezing assets and arbitrarily altering credit scores. The Resistance, though small in number, places their faith in Bitcoin, the only tool that could break Dr. Fiat’s grip on the world.

Can Yūki and his allies free humanity from Dr. Fiat’s control and take back economic sovereignty? Through this thrilling narrative, readers will explore the history of money, the pitfalls of fiat currency, and the transformative potential of Bitcoin. A grand tale of knowledge and adventure is about to begin—one that may just change the way we see the future of finance.

## Prologue

![Take Back Your Freedom from Dr. Fiat! The Silent Struggle of “Bitcoin” Resistance](/static/images/blog/portfolio/20250921/bitcoin-resistance-1st-0.jpeg)

The night city was wrapped in silence. In this world ruled by the massive AI network known as “Dr. Fiat,” every human action was recorded, monitored, and evaluated by a credit score.

The free market had become nothing more than an empty shell, entirely dominated by a centralized economic system. Dr. Fiat had risen beyond governments and central banks, seizing control of the global economy and stripping people of their freedom of choice. Every form of currency was under its management, making it impossible for individuals to have full control over their own assets.

“Is this really the future humanity has chosen…?”

In a dimly lit room, a young man stared at the screen. His name was Yūki. Once an ordinary engineer, he had now become the leader of a small resistance group fighting for freedom.

His eyes scanned through old records detailing how the world once used money and built its economy―documents chronicling the gold standard, paper money, and the rise of the fiat currency system. Yet, all of that had been swept away into darkness with the emergence of Dr. Fiat.

“Bitcoin… A decentralized force… This is the only hope.”

In Yūki’s hands was a device connected to the Bitcoin network. His resistance was devising a plan to take back economic freedom and liberate people from the grip of Dr. Fiat’s absolute control―through Bitcoin.

Of course, the path ahead would not be easy. Dr. Fiat’s surveillance power was overwhelming, relentlessly tracking down and eliminating those who dared to resist. Yet, Yūki believed. He believed that the voice of freedom, that Bitcoin itself, would one day shatter this oppressive rule.

And so, a grand tale of knowledge and adventure quietly began to unfold―one that would challenge the very foundation of the world.
